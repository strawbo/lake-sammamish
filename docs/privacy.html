Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 12, 2026

Lake Sammamish ("the App") is a free app that displays real-time swimming and boating conditions for Lake Sammamish, Washington. Your privacy is important to us. This policy explains what data the App accesses and how it is used.

Location Data

The App requests access to your device's location only to display your position on the Lake Sammamish map. Your location is used entirely on-device and is never transmitted, stored, or shared with any server, third party, or analytics service. If you deny location permission, the App functions normally — you simply won't see your position on the map.

Data We Collect

None. The App does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. There are no user accounts, no analytics, no tracking, no cookies, and no advertising.

Data Sources

The App fetches publicly available environmental data from:

  • King County — Lake buoy measurements (water temperature, turbidity, meteorological data)
  • Open-Meteo — Weather forecasts and air quality data

This data is pre-processed and served as static JSON files from GitHub Pages. No personal information is included in these requests.

Data Caching

The App caches environmental data locally on your device so it remains available offline. This cache contains only lake conditions data — never personal information. It is stored in the App's private sandbox and is deleted when you uninstall the App.

Third-Party Services

The App does not integrate any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

Children's Privacy

The App does not collect personal data from anyone, including children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted at this URL. Continued use of the App constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

Contact

Questions about this privacy policy can be directed to the developer via the App's support page on the App Store.
